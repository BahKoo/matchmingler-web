Awesome Twosome

Upcoming Events

What is this all about?

This is a unique concept where you team up with anyone of any age and compete in your combined age group.

Add your age and your partner's age to determine your age category. You then pick your level (from 2.5 to 4.5). Men's, women's and mixed events will be offered.

This is neat!

This format would open the door to a number of different pairings. For example a parent can team up with a child or a grandmother or grandfather can team up with a grandchild.

The combinations that would be formed would make the tournament full of surprises. Two 40 year olds could end up playing against a team consisting of a 60 year old and a 28 year old (both would fall within the 75-99 combined age category). There is no advantage to either side providing that both teams pick the appropriate skill level category (from 2.5 to level 4.5).

When will this take place?

There will be multiple tournaments offered in 2025 depending on demand. As soon as one category has 8 teams it's a GO and a date will be chosen.

The plan is to have these on weekends to allow everyone a chance to participate.

Cost

The cost is $60 per team - payable only when a date has been chosen and providing the date chosen is acceptable to you.
