What is Classic Bracket?

Classic Bracket is a traditional tournament similar to other tournaments you may have participated in previously.

Tournaments normally have the following characteristics:

Gender

Sometimes there are categories available for men, other times women. There are also mixed categories.

Levels

Levels are used to identify a skill level, normally ranging from 2.0 to 5.0. These levels are actually called DUPR ratings. After having played in a tournament at a 3.0 level or 3.5 level for example you will quickly learn if that level of play was too easy or too difficult for you and your partner. In a subsequent tournament you might move up (if the level you chose was too easy) or move down.

Age

Sometimes an event may give you an option to select an age category. For example there could be an over 50 category that you can choose where both partners need to be over the age of 50. There may be a 65+ category. The purpose of an age category is to allow the older folks to play amongst themselves and not have to compete against those quick youngsters!
